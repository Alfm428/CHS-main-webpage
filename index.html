Hello, we are from Christian Heritage School−an independent Christian school in Edwall, WA. As we are located in a rural area, we provide isolated, focused Christian education and a healthy environment. Check us out here and contact us for more information!

Small community

We have only enrolled about 5 to 6 students per class so far. Our small community setting of the school makes everyone connected and attached to each other.

Isolated location

Students of our school will not have any access to risky environments. Rural location of the school provide many oppertunities to engage with clean, natural setting aroung the school.

Dedicated teachers

As we have a small-community-like student body, students have many oppertunities to get closer to their teachers. Our teachers put the relationship with students in priority.

Affordable private education

We have a very affordable pricing compare to any other private school in US. Additonally, 10% Family Income Cap provides necessary finencial aid for those in need.
